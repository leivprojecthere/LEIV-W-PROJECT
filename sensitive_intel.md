## 📊 Sensitivity Scale (1–10)

Intel submissions are classified by sensitivity. The higher the score, the stricter the rules.  
All submissions must be sent to: **leivhere@tutamail.com**

---

### Tier 1–3: Low Sensitivity
- Definition: Public or semi‑public info (OSINT, recon, non‑classified).
- Rules:
  - Encryption optional.
  - Metadata stripping recommended.
- Submission: Send to **leivhere@tutamail.com**
- Quote: *“Knowledge is power.” — Francis Bacon*

---

### Tier 4–6: Medium Sensitivity
- Definition: Internal leaks, corporate surveillance, non‑public but not life‑threatening.
- Rules:
  - Encryption required.
  - Metadata stripping mandatory.
- Submission: Send to **leivhere@tutamail.com**
- Quote: *“The people who cast the votes decide nothing. The people who count the votes decide everything.” — Joseph Stalin*

---

### Tier 7–8: High Sensitivity
- Definition: Government surveillance, censorship, whistleblowing with risk to source.
- Rules:
  - Strong encryption required.
  - Metadata stripped.
  - Anonymous submission enforced.
- Submission: Send to **leivhere@tutamail.com**
- Quote: *“In a time of deceit, telling the truth is a revolutionary act.” — George Orwell*

---

### Tier 9–10: Critical Sensitivity
- Definition: State secrets, military surveillance, intel that could endanger lives.
- Rules:
  - Mandatory encryption.
  - Metadata stripped.
  - Anonymous submission only.
  - Strict review before release.
  - **Photos recommended** — but ONLY if stripped of all metadata (EXIF, GPS, author tags). Raw metadata will expose you.
- Submission: Send to **leivhere@tutamail.com**
- Quote: *“Political power grows out of the barrel of a gun.” — Mao Zedong*

---

## 📜 OPSEC Submission Rules

- **Encryption** → Required for Tier 4–10. Use GPG or VeraCrypt containers.  
- **Metadata removal** → Mandatory for Tier 4–10. Strip EXIF, GPS, author tags, timestamps.  
- **Anonymous submission** → Required for Tier 7–10. Use Tor or VPN when sending.  
- **Photos for Tier 9–10** → Allowed only if metadata‑free. Never send raw images with EXIF data.  
- **Strict review** → All intel above Tier 5 must be verified before publication.  
- **Meritocracy** → Identity irrelevant; only intel quality matters.
