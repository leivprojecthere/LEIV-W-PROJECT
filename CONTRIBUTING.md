# Contributing Guidelines

We welcome contributions of **anonymous intelligence files** and **OSINT research notes** to the LEIV‑W Project.  
To protect your privacy and maintain operational security, please follow these rules carefully:

---

## Submission Rules
1. **Anonymous Only**  
   - All intel submissions must be made from a **burner GitHub account**.  
   - Do not use your personal or long‑term GitHub identity.  

2. **Privacy First**  
   - After submitting your pull request, **delete your burner account**.  
   - This ensures no long‑term linkage between your identity and the contribution.  

3. **Content Requirements**  
   - Submissions must contain **no Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**.  
   - No doxxing, targeting individuals, or illegal exploitation.  
   - Contributions should focus on **research, OSINT workflows, and anonymized intel**.  
   - **False or fabricated information will be deleted and rejected.**  

4. **Ethics & Legality**  
   - All work must comply with international cybercrime frameworks (IC3, FBI, INTERPOL).  
   - This project is strictly for **educational and research purposes**.  

---

## Review Process
- Submitted intel will be reviewed for compliance with these rules.  
- If accepted, it will be published in the repo under `/intel`.  
- Non‑compliant or false submissions will be **rejected without exception**.  

---

## Final Note
Your privacy matters.  
By using burner accounts and deleting them after submission, you help ensure that **LEIV‑W Project** remains a secure, ethical, and anonymous research framework.
