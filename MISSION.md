# LEIV‑W Mission Statement

## 🌍 Purpose
LEIV‑W exists to expose the **dark side of the world** — the corruption, hidden truths, and systemic abuses that governments and corporations try to sugar‑coat.  
We do this without hosting raw leaks or naming real entities, ensuring resilience and protection for contributors.

## 🛡️ Core Principles
- **[Anonymization](ca://s?q=Whistleblowing_anonymization)** → All submissions are stripped of names, identifiers, and metadata. Outsiders see only “gov/corp/thing.”  
- **[Neutral framework](ca://s?q=Whistleblowing_frameworks)** → LEIV‑W is a knowledge hub, not a battlefield. It teaches whistleblowing methods without fueling intel wars.  
- **[Resilience](ca://s?q=Docker_horizontal_scaling)** → Dockerized deployments, decentralized mirrors, and fallback hosting ensure survival against DDoS or takedowns.  
- **[Freedom of speech](ca://s?q=Whistleblowing_philosophy)** → Guides, philosophy, and anonymized workflows are protected speech.  

## 🚫 What We Avoid
- **No raw leaks** → We do not host classified files or identifiable documents.  
- **No naming governments/corporations** → Prevents contributors from being targeted and denies intel agencies leverage for counter‑intelligence.  
- **No social media presence** → Social platforms are fragile under government pressure. GitHub (under Microsoft) provides stronger resilience.  

## ⚖️ Strategic Shield
- Governments cannot target us without admitting leaks are real.  
- Intel agencies may see the info, but it is **hard to link** to any specific government or corporation.  
- Any takedown attempt would contradict platform rules, exposing hypocrisy and sparking backlash.  

## 🌐 Vision
LEIV‑W is **Tor in real life**: anonymized, decentralized, and resistant to pressure.  
We empower whistleblowers, protect contributors, and ensure the world sees reality — not the sugar‑coated version.
